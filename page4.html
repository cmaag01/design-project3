    It can be hard to remeber what skincare does what for your skin. This is just a little reminder in case you ever forget.




    Cleanser:






    Cleanser in an essential part of skincare, and probably the most important. It's mainly used to clean off the dirt of the day and even though you can't see it, it's stille there. It can also be used to hydrate, moisturise, clean pores, and much more.











    Moisturiser:






    Moisturiser helps keep your skin hydrated. It works well on dry skin to keep it moisturised, but you can also use it on normal skin to prevent dryness.











    Serum:






Serums can have may different roles and do many different things. For example, anti-aging, hydrating, glowing, and many more. Since there are so many options, you need to make sure that you get one sutable for your skintype.











      Toner:






      Toner is often used to even out the look of the skin, ecspecialy reduce pores. It can be used to make your skin apear more glowy or even younger.
